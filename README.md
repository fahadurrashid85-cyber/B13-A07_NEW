Project Name: KeenKeeper
Project Description: Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most.
Tech Stack: Next.js, Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI, Lucide React.
Features:
Dashboard: Overview of your connections, their status, and recent interactions.
Friends Section: List of all your friends with their details, tags, and bio.
Add Friends Page: Form to add new friends with their information.
Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on different screen sizes.
Quick Stats: Quick statistics of your connections.
